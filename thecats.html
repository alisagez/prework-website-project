The Cats

This page contains information about three wonderful cats that are near and dear to Alisa's heart.



Oos

Oos is a very curious cat. He loves shadows and playing in the bathtub, sleeping on pillows (if those are placed on top of a human sitting on the couch), and playing with string.



Gosha

Gosha is a very sweet cat. He loves to eat and hang out with the people that he knows (he is very cautious when he meets new people). He also likes running around the house and playing with Oos.



Beatrix

Beatrix is a princess! She loves playing outside, sleeping next to a space heater, and eating fancy treats. She is a very social cat around people, but she does not like other animals.
